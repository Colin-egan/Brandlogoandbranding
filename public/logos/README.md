## Usage Guidelines

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum padding equal to the height of the lever arm (the horizontal line extending left from the center) around all sides of the logo.

### Minimum Sizes
- **Digital**: Never display smaller than 32px × 32px
- **Print**: Never display smaller than 0.5" × 0.5"
- **Favicon**: 16px is acceptable only for browser favicons

### Color Modifications
Do not alter logo colors or create custom color variants. Use only the provided versions:
- Dark backgrounds: Use primary-dark or reversed-blue
- Light backgrounds: Use primary-light
- Transparent: Use icon-only variants

### Prohibited Uses
- ❌ Do not stretch, rotate, or skew the logo
- ❌ Do not apply filters, shadows, or effects
- ❌ Do not change colors or remix elements
- ❌ Do not place on busy backgrounds that reduce contrast
- ❌ Always maintain 1:1 aspect ratio

## Typography

**Display (Wordmark):**
- Font: Space Grotesk Black (900 weight)
- Letter spacing: +18%
- Transform: Uppercase

**Tagline:**
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: Relative to wordmark (typically 14-16% of wordmark height)
- Letter spacing: +55%
- Transform: Uppercase
- Color: Muted (#4A6080)

**Body Text:**
- Font: Inter (400 regular, 600 semibold)
- Line height: 1.6-1.7 for comfortable reading
