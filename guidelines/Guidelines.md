# Torque Brand Guidelines

**Tagline:** Leverage Your Network  
**Product:** Agentic networking platform for LinkedIn connection analysis and management

---

## Brand Essence

Torque represents the convergence of mechanical precision and network intelligence. The brand identity balances technical sophistication with approachable design, conveying power, leverage, and connection.

### Brand Attributes
- **Powerful** — Like torque applies force to create motion
- **Connected** — Network-first thinking
- **Intelligent** — Agentic, analytical, data-driven
- **Professional** — Enterprise-grade quality
- **Modern** — Clean, technical aesthetic

---

## Logo System

### The Mark

The Torque logomark is a geometric composition that reads as both:
1. **Mechanical leverage** — A wrench socket with lever arm extending from the center pivot
2. **Network graph** — Nodes connected through a central hub

**Key Elements:**
- **C-shaped arc** — The main circular band (270° sweep, 90° gap on right)
- **Lever arm** — Horizontal line extending left from center
- **Center pivot** — Ring detail at the focal point
- **Network nodes** — White dots at arc endpoints and lever tip
- **Connection lines** — Subtle web inside the arc, suggesting network topology

### Logo Variants

**Primary Dark** (Default)
- Background: Surface (#0C1219)
- Accent: Electric Blue (#2563EB)
- Nodes: White (#FFFFFF)
- Use: Dark UI, dark marketing backgrounds

**Primary Light**
- Background: Light gray (#EEF2F7)
- Accent: Electric Blue (#2563EB)
- Nodes: Deep Navy (#0C1219)
- Use: Light UI, light marketing backgrounds, print on white

**Reversed Blue**
- Background: Electric Blue (#2563EB)
- Accent: White (#FFFFFF)
- Nodes: Electric Blue (same as bg)
- Use: High-impact moments, CTAs, badges

**Icon-Only** (Transparent)
- Use for: Favicons, app icons, small UI contexts where background is provided

### Wordmark

**Horizontal Lockup:**
- Logo mark on left
- "TORQUE" wordmark in Space Grotesk Black, uppercase, +18% letter spacing
- Tagline "LEVERAGE YOUR NETWORK" below in Inter Regular, +55% letter spacing, uppercase, muted color

**Spacing:**
- Gap between mark and wordmark: 0.8× logo height
- Gap between wordmark and tagline: 0.15× wordmark height

## Typography

### Display Typography

**Space Grotesk Black**
- Weight: 900 (Black)
- Use: Wordmark, hero headings, major CTAs
- Letter spacing: +14% to +22%
- Transform: Uppercase
- Example: `TORQUE`, `UNLOCK YOUR NETWORK`

### Body Typography

**Inter**
- **Headings:** 600 (Semibold), tight tracking (-0.02em)
- **Body:** 400 (Regular), relaxed line height (1.6-1.7)
- **Captions:** 400 (Regular), wide tracking (+0.35em) uppercase for metadata/tags

### Hierarchy

```
Display (48-72px) — Space Grotesk Black, uppercase, wide
H1 (32-40px) — Inter Semibold
H2 (24-28px) — Inter Semibold
H3 (18-20px) — Inter Semibold
Body (14-16px) — Inter Regular
Caption (11-12px) — Inter Regular, uppercase, wide tracking
```

## Spacing & Layout

### Grid System
- Base unit: 4px
- Common increments: 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px

### Logo Clear Space
- Minimum padding: Equal to the height of the lever arm
- Measure from the outermost element (lever tip or arc edge)

### Corner Radius
- Small (buttons, inputs): 8-12px
- Medium (cards): 16-20px
- Large (containers): 24-32px

---

## Logo Usage Rules

### ✅ Do:
- Use provided SVG files without modification
- Maintain 1:1 aspect ratio
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background
- Use appropriate variant for context (dark/light/reversed)
- Respect minimum size (32px digital, 0.5" print)
- Provide clear space around logo

### ❌ Don't:
- Change logo colors
- Stretch, rotate, or skew
- Add effects (shadows, glows, gradients on logo itself)
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Create new color variants
- Separate or rearrange logo elements
- Use wordmark without the mark
- Display smaller than minimum size
